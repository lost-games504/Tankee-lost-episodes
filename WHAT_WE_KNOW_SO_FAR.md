What We Know So Far
A summary of confirmed and strongly remembered details about the lost Tankee dinosaur series (2019–2021)

1. Platform & Availability
The series appeared exclusively on the Tankee app and HappyKids TV.

It was never uploaded to YouTube or mirrored on the creator’s personal channel.

It existed during the 2019–2021 content era.

In 2022, Tankee removed all pre‑2022 content, causing the series to disappear completely.

No official archives, backups, or screenshots have surfaced.

2. Visual Style
The dinosaurs were Jurassic Park–style, with realistic, high‑quality 3D models.

The world was bright, colorful, and kid‑friendly.

The environment was fully 3D and not Minecraft, Roblox, or PixARK.

The series likely used a custom engine or custom modded world unique to Tankee’s early content.

3. Characters & Dinosaurs
The following dinosaurs are strongly remembered:

Gallimimus (“George”)

Spinosaurus (“Riptide”)

Brachiosaurus

Woolly Mammoth (unlockable)

The show also focused on The Big Three:

T. rex

Triceratops

Brachiosaurus

The original Tankee host guided viewers through the adventure.

4. Episode Structure & Features
Although it was a video series, it included game‑like elements:

DNA Extractor machine

Embryo Mixer

Incubator

Fossil collecting

Dinosaur raising

A wooden barn with pens

A Jurassic‑style jeep

Building dinosaur statues

Completing quests and objectives

5. Why It’s Lost
Tankee’s early content was app‑only and never mirrored.

The 2022 purge deleted the entire early catalog.

Children watching at the time were too young to archive episodes.

No known footage, thumbnails, or metadata remain.
