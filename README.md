# Tankee-lost-episodes
This repository documents a dinosaur adventure series that appeared exclusively on the Tankee app and HappyKids TV between 2019–2021. The series featured Jurassic Park–style dinosaurs, high‑quality 3D models, and a bright, kid‑friendly prehistoric world. It included elements such as DNA extraction, embryo mixing, incubation, a wooden barn, and a Jurassic‑style jeep. The original Tankee host guided viewers through exploring the world, collecting fossils, crafting dinosaur embryos, and raising baby dinosaurs.

The series was never uploaded to YouTube, and during Tankee’s 2022 catalog purge, all pre‑2022 content was permanently removed. No official archives, mirrors, or backups of this dinosaur series are known to exist. As of now, it is considered lost media.

This project exists to preserve the memory of the series and gather information from anyone who remembers it.
