How to Help the Search
A guide for anyone who may remember or have evidence of the lost Tankee dinosaur series (2019–2021)

1. Share Any Memories You Have
Even small details matter. If you watched Tankee between 2019–2021, please share anything you remember about:

dinosaurs

characters

episode plots

locations

machines (DNA Extractor, Embryo Mixer, Incubator)

the barn or jeep

the host

the world’s look and feel

A single sentence can help confirm important details.

2. Look for Old Screenshots or Photos
Many kids accidentally took screenshots on tablets or phones. If you still have an old device from that time, check:

your photo gallery

screenshots folder

cloud backups (Google Photos, iCloud)

shared albums

old SD cards

Even one blurry image would be extremely valuable.

3. Search for Old Tankee APKs
If you used Android during 2019–2021, you might still have:

old APK files

cached app data

backups from older phones

downloads from third‑party app stores

Early Tankee APKs may contain thumbnails, metadata, or leftover assets.

4. Check Family Devices
Parents often keep old phones or tablets in drawers. These may still contain:

installed versions of Tankee

screenshots

cached thumbnails

video fragments

Ask family members if they still have devices from that era.

5. Share Anything You Find
If you discover:

screenshots

clips

APKs

thumbnails

descriptions

episode titles

memories

Please post them in the comments or contact the repository owner.
All contributions help.

6. Spread the Word
If you know anyone who used Tankee during 2019–2021, let them know about this search. Lost media is often found because someone remembers something years later.

7. Respect Privacy & Safety
Only share files you own and are comfortable sharing. Do not attempt unsafe downloads or suspicious APK sites.
