FAQ – Tankee Dino Adventure Lost Media
What is this project?
This repository documents a dinosaur adventure series that appeared on the Tankee app and HappyKids TV between 2019–2021. The series featured realistic Jurassic Park–style dinosaurs, high‑quality 3D models, and a kid‑friendly prehistoric world. It was deleted during Tankee’s 2022 catalog purge and is now considered lost media.

Why is the series lost?
The Tankee app hosted exclusive content that was never mirrored to YouTube or other platforms. When Tankee removed all pre‑2022 content, the dinosaur series disappeared completely. No official archives, screenshots, or video clips have surfaced.

Was this a game or a video series?
It was a video series, but it included game‑like elements such as DNA crafting machines, embryo mixing, incubation, fossil collecting, and dinosaur raising. These features suggest the creators used a custom engine or modded world.

Who was the host?
The original Tankee host (the same girl featured in other Tankee content) guided viewers through exploring the dinosaur world, crafting embryos, and raising baby dinosaurs.

Which dinosaurs were in the series?
Strongly remembered dinosaurs include:

Gallimimus (“George”)

Spinosaurus (“Riptide”)

Triceratops

Brachiosaurus

Stegosaurus

Ankylosaurus

Dilophosaurus

Woolly Mammoth

The show also focused on The Big Three: T. rex, Triceratops, and Brachiosaurus.

Why is there no footage?
Most viewers were young children who didn’t record or archive episodes. The series was app‑exclusive, and Tankee did not provide download options. Once the purge happened, the content vanished.
